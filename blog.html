NWHacks

February 2, 2019

NWHacks finished last weekend, and WOAH that was exciting. Not only was there hacking, but there were fun activities, engagement in the slack channels, and overall it was just GREAT!! For a more in depth you can check the video I posted on our YouTube channel. One exciting update since that video, is that our project is a winner! We got top 20, winning the Wolfram award. Honestly, that was pretty surprising, but that means, if we had a finished and functioning prototype, we might have even gotten top five. Maybe a simple UI, some better notification actions, and we'd have a contender for first. Just the prospect is pretty damn cool. Go check out the vlog at the link below.
